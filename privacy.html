Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 25, 2026

Mokie ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by Mokie.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, log a transaction, or contact us for support.

  • Account Information: If you use cloud sync features, we may collect your email address and authentication details.
  • Financial Data: We store the transaction data (amounts, categories, notes) you enter locally on your device or securely in our cloud database if sync is enabled.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • Sync your data across devices (if enabled).
  • Respond to your comments and questions.

3. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Your financial data is yours; we do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at Support.
